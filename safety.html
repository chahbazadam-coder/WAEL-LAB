✅ General lab rules

  • No lone working with lasers, high voltage, or hazardous chemicals.
  • Wear safety glasses / laser goggles rated for the source in use.
  • Closed shoes and lab coat in wet/chemical areas.
  • Know where the nearest exit, eyewash, and extinguisher are.
  • Label every container — contents, your name, date.
  • No food or drink at the benches.
  • Return tools and log any equipment fault on the Machines page.
  • Solvents (acetone, IPA) only in the fume hood, away from ignition.
  • Tie back long hair and remove loose sleeves near rotating equipment.
  • Complete required training before using any Class 3B/4 laser.
