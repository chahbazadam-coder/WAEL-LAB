What we do

Research areas

Three connected pillars — we combine them to build complete lab-on-chip and photonic systems.

Microfluidics

Lab-on-chip devices, droplet systems, and PDMS fabrication for precise control of tiny fluid volumes.

Optics & Lasers

Laser-based measurement, imaging, and photonic components for probing and manipulating micro-systems.

Electronics

Custom control electronics, sensors, and data acquisition that tie the fluidics and optics together.
